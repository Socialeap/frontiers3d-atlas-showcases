Even Hotel Times Square S. New York, NY
This sleek modern hotel is a 2-minute walk from 34th Street-Penn Station subway station, a 13-minute walk from the Empire State Building and less than a mile from the High Line, an elevated walkway. Polished rooms offer free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, plus minifridges and coffeemakers. They also include exercise balls, yoga mats and fitness training videos. Amenities include a casual bar/restaurant with light bites and to-go fare, along with a 24-hour gym.
